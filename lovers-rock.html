TAILORING YOUR COACHING FOCUS

Lovers' Rock focuses on male dancehall flow, drawing from mid-2010s new-school dancehall movement, with melodic Dancehall tracks provide a tempo constraint, allowing dancers to refine execution in choreography.

Classes break down how popular male dancehall steps, grooves, and transitions from that era translate onto slower rhythms, maintaining a badman frame, grounded base, and continuous motion.

Lovers' Rock is a genre of reggae, but when applied to dancehall, it includes a reference to well known Dancehall singers' melodic tracks (think Dexta, Popcaan, Nigy Boy) that lend to new school choreography.

In this class, you'll learn:

  • New School male dancehall moves, adapted to choreography
  • Choreography fluency
  • Male dancehall flow at slow to mid tempo
  • Continuous movement between steps & finishing movement
  • Weight placement, grounding, and lower-body control
