About

Meet Chuby Dice

Chuby Dice (Desmond Malcolm) is a Jamaican-born Dancehall artist from Spanish Town, now based in Naarm (Melbourne).

Spanish Town is one of the pillars of Dancehall culture — shaped by Black diasporic history, sound-system energy, and everyday resilience. For Chuby, Dancehall isn't imported or abstract. It's lived.

That's why his teaching goes beyond studio walls. You'll find him on the streets, on stages, in community spaces, and in classrooms — bringing Dancehall to wherever people gather.

Dancehall is for everyone, but it must always stay rooted in where it comes from.
