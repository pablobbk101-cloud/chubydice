TAILORING YOUR COACHING FOCUS

March Back In Time focuses on Black Dice movement history, tracing the evolution of the crew's style from Central Village, Spain Town, through to its continued influence on modern dancehall.

In this series, Chuby teaches how Black Dice has developed and carried movement vocabulary across eras, from mid-school foundations from Black Blingaz, to new-school execution. The emphasis is on understanding how early Black Dice steps remain relevant by forming the base for later movements like Free Kick.

Chuby provides personal history on how members of Black Dice maintained consistency in groove, stance, and rhythm while adapting to each other and evolving music styles as well as changing party environments. The focus is on party steps, repetition, and connection through Dancehall.

In this class, you'll master:

  • Core Black Dice history & moves
  • The transition from mid-school to new-school dancehall mechanics
  • Grounding, bounce, and rhythmic consistency across eras
  • How foundational steps evolved into modern variation
  • Flow, stamina, and repetition used in party and crew settings
