TAILORING YOUR COACHING FOCUS

Motherland reflects on the newest of New School moves, tracing lineages and building Dancehall vocabulary.

In the context of Jamaican Dancehall, "mother" is not just care. It is structure, discipline, and sacrifice, transforming pickney youth.

In this course, Chuby takes you through both foundation and new school Dancehall. Not separating them, but showing how the same root carries forward.

In this Series, you'll cover:

  • Sharper execution and control
  • Timing and musical awareness — an ability to recognise sub-genres of Dancehall
  • The Patois behind the dance — and how Dancehall music explains ordinary relationships
